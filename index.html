Engineering Services

We transform your engineering designs into precise, construction-ready deliverables using industry-leading tools and workflows.

CAD

CAD Drafting & Documentation

Production of precise, standards-compliant 2D plans, cross sections, long sections, detail sheets, and title blocks with attention to dimensional accuracy.

3D

Civil 3D Modelling

Advanced surface, corridor, and pipe network modelling based on your design parameters, producing outputs including profiles, sections, and quantity take-offs.

BIM

BIM Federation & Clash Detection

Multi-discipline model assembly and coordination in Navisworks for comprehensive clash analysis with structured issue tracking and resolution workflows.

VIZ

InfraWorks Visualisations

Creation of visually rich concept models and animations using your engineering data — optimized for stakeholder presentations and preliminary design reviews.

GEO

Earthworks & Grading Models

Comprehensive platform shaping to cut/fill balance and surface exports — built with precision directly from your terrain and layout specifications.

ROW

Road Geometry & Utility Layouts

Accurate modelling of road centerlines, alignments, and utility routes as per your design drawings, optimized for Civil 3D or GIS-compatible outputs.

WTR

Three Water Pipe Networks

Complete modelling of water supply, wastewater, and stormwater networks with detailed pipe layouts, sizing, and connectivity documentation to industry standards.

PLN

Network Plans

Detailed plan views of utility networks showing pipe alignments, node locations, valve placements, and connection points with comprehensive annotation and dimensioning.

PRF

Profiles for Gravity Systems

Precise longitudinal profiles for gravity-fed systems showing pipe inverts, ground levels, gradients, and hydraulic grade lines to ensure optimal flow performance.

RSM

Rising Main Profiles

Specialized profiles for pressurized rising mains including pump station connections, air valve locations, scour points, and pressure analysis documentation.
